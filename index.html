ABOUT THE GAME

What is On The Clock?

On The Clock is a daily sports trivia game that tests your knowledge across the major leagues. Every day, all players get the same 10 questions — compete with friends and climb the leaderboard!

How To Play

Type your answer before the 25-second timer runs out. Stuck? Use one of your 2 multiple choice lifelines to see four possible answers instead.

Difficulty Levels

RookieQuestions 1-3 • Easier questions to warm up
VeteranQuestions 4-7 • Mid-level challenge
All-ProQuestions 8-10 • Only true fans know these

Sports Covered

NBA, MLB, NFL, NHL, NCAA Football, and NCAA Basketball — with questions spanning from 1980 to present day.

Scoring & Streaks

Play daily to build your streak! Get a perfect 10/10 to earn the 👑 crown badge. Track your stats, earn achievements, and see how you stack up on the leaderboard.
